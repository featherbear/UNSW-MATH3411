Cyclotomic sets are sets whose numbers will repeat ($mod n$) 

i.e in $GF(16) ( which is $mod 15$ )...  
$K_1 = {1, 2, 4, 8}  
$K_3 = {3, 6, 12 , 9}  
etc...

Cyclotomic if $gcd(a, b) == 1$
